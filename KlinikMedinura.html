Common Concerns & Care Tips

Need help figuring out what to do? Let us guide you.

Child's Fever

Worried about your child's temperature?

When to Visit:
  • Fever above 39°C
  • Lasts more than 3 days
  • With severe symptoms
Home Care:
  • Cool compress bath
  • Plenty of fluids
  • Light, easy meals
Minor Wounds

Cuts, scrapes, or small injuries at home?

When to Visit:
  • Deep or large cuts
  • Won't stop bleeding
  • Signs of infection
Home Care:
  • Clean with soap & water
  • Apply antibiotic cream
  • Cover with bandage
Cough & Cold

Wondering if it's just a cold or something more?

When to Visit:
  • Cough lasting 2+ weeks
  • High fever with cough
  • Difficulty breathing
Home Care:
  • Stay hydrated
  • Use a humidifier
  • Gargle with salt water
